Victoria

A New Musical

Victoria: A New Musical tells the story of Jeffery Brown who suffers a stroke that takes away his color vision. 10 years later, he gets involved with an Alzheimer's research drug, Victoria, which allows him to relive his memories and re-experience them again in full color. With this new drug comes new complications, new stories to tell, and new demons to face.

Victoria: A New Musical was originally produced at the Cambridge YMCA in Cambridge, MA, running July 27-29, 2017. Music, Book, Lyrics written by Prosper Fiske, Directed by Sarah Mickey, Music Directed and Orchestrated by Utsav Bhargava.
